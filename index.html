Beetroot (Beta vulgaris)

A bundle of organic beets from a local farm food co-op program.

55 to 65 days are needed from germination to harvest of the root.

The usually deeo purple roots of beetroot are eaten either grilled, boiled or roasted as a cooked vegetable, cold as a salad after cooking and adding oil and vinigar, or raw and shredded, either alone or combined with any salad vegetable.
